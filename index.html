What is xLungs?

Our problem

The aim of our project is to create tools for automated diagnosis of various lungs diseases to support radiologists in their work.

Our data

We work with our partners to create the largest database of patients in Poland, consisting of anonymised tabular data, x-rays and CT scans.

Our methods

Knowing the pros and cons of ML systems, we do not only wish to deliver high-performing solution, but also fully explainable and reliable one.

Funding

Work on this project is financially supported by the NCBiR grant INFOSTRATEG-I/0022/2021-00

Who we are?

We highly believe that the best projects are done in interdisciplinary teams. This is why we combine knowledge of:

Data & ML Experts

From statisticians to deep learning engineers, we want to explore the broadest range of solutions. We also use our years-long Explainable AI expertise to assure our models are fully interpretable.

Medical Experts

We combine the knowledge of radiologists and diagnosticians to ensure we fully understand the problems we face. They help us in data annotation, testing the hypotheses and verifying that our solutions can be used by them in the future.

We deliver

Data

In the first stage, we cleaned and anonymised files of more than 30 thousand patients. We later shared portion of our data during Lungs Decoded Challenge.

Research

In stage number 2, our goal is to deliver high-performing and explainable machine learning models to classify lungs diseases from CT scans, x-rays and patients’ history.

Productisation

Finally, we want to test best solutions with medical teams to adjust them specifically to their needs and offer them tools to make their day-to-day work more efficient.
